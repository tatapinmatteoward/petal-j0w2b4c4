## Crafting Your Own Autumn Ritual

Ready to create your own Blossom & Brew-inspired ritual? Here's a simple recipe to try at home:

### Ingredients (Serves 1):
- 1 tsp loose chamomile flowers
- ½ tsp dried hibiscus
- A pinch of ground cinnamon
- Hot water (not boiling, around 90°C)
- Optional: Honey and a lemon slice

### Instructions:
1. Place the herbs in a teapot or infuser.
2. Pour hot water over them and steep for 5 minutes.
3. Strain and sweeten to taste. Garnish with lemon for an extra zing.

Pair it with our signature pumpkin spice scones for the ultimate fall treat. This blend not only warms the body but also soothes the mind, making it ideal for meditation or cozy reading sessions.
